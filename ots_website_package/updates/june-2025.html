Let me tell you that I am the father of an ex member of the OTS 2025, first of all I want to thank the Devon authorities who intervened in the accident of Tavistock for worrying and caring for and protecting the occupants of the van that was destroyed among the where was my son. As a result of this fact, the authorities, when investigating the occupants of the truck, realized that the truck did not have the necessary documentation to drive. When taking the declarations, they demonstrate that in three consecutive days they had slept only 4 hours, reason more than enough for this outcome. this lack of rest was due to Mr Nicolas Andres Guzman who calls himself Captain Nicholas Moffat forced them to move in the vans driven by the same musicians and soundists who were collapsed from fatigue and hunger, at the time of the accident about a week they counted as food (lunch, snack and dinner) a bag of cereals without sugar, almost no longer had money to buy their food. In the places where they played, that provided some food the Captain (almost never present but if by phone) gave orders to his core (Patricio Manifesto, Lazaro Infante, Ignacio Camiroaga, Brisa Vigna, Federico Montailbet) who demanded the girtos to disarm quickly and charge everything to get out.
