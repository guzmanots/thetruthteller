Members of Old Time Sailors arrested. It has come to our attention that members of The Old Time Sailors were arrested by Devon and Cornwall Police on the morning of Monday 19th May on suspected Modern Day Slavery charges. This was a result of an accident in the early morning at Tiverton, Devon between members of The Old Time Sailors van and a heavy goods vehicle. Devonlive.com

Could this accident been a result of playing at The Berkeley Arms in Wymondham until 10:00PM, then packing up the vans and driving through the night back to Gullworthy so that they could perform again the next day at the Cornishman near Newquay?
